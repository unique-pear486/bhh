Controls

  • If you ever get stuck just refresh (F5)- the server saves everything
  • Hit the "h"-key to get this help (so long as you're not in the menus).
  • Click and drag to move tokens, tiles and markers around
  • Shift-click to Show tokens or tiles fullscreen
  • Ctrl-click to delete tokens or tiles
  • Ctrl+Shift-click to rotate tokens or tiles by 90 degrees

  • Click on the centre of your Character's image to add a token to the Current floor. This is useful for going up and down stairs.


  • To change floors, choose the initial on the left: B for basement, G for ground and U for upper
  • Click on the dice symbol to roll the dice
  • Click on the search button then type part of a name to draw specific cards, tiles or tokens. Click on one of the search results to add it.

  • Click on the tile deck to draw a new tile. You may choose to limit your selection to one floor by hovering over that floor before clicking
